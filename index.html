A Dynamic D&D Labyrinth

Party (Left Click)
Minotaur (Right Click)
How to play: Hover near the edges of the board to reveal insertion arrows. Click an arrow to shift that row or column. A random tile will be inserted from your hidden spare, and the tile pushed out becomes the new spare (also randomly rotated).

Place tokens: Left-click on a tile to place the party (red token). Right-click to place the minotaur (black token). Tokens move with the tiles when shifting!
